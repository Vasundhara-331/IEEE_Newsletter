Welcome to IEEE LaunchPad Kerala

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala stands as the premier technology incubation and innovation hub in Kerala, fostering the next generation of tech entrepreneurs and innovators. As part of the globally recognized IEEE organization, we bridge the gap between academic excellence and industry innovation.

Our Mission: To nurture technological innovation, provide mentorship to budding entrepreneurs, and create a vibrant ecosystem where groundbreaking ideas transform into successful ventures that drive economic growth and technological advancement in Kerala.

Through our comprehensive programs, we offer mentorship, funding opportunities, technical resources, and industry connections to help startups and innovators turn their visionary ideas into market-ready solutions.

Event Roadmap 2024-25

Our comprehensive event roadmap is designed to provide continuous learning, networking, and growth opportunities throughout the year. Each phase builds upon the previous, creating a holistic development journey for our community.

Phase 1: Foundation Building (June - August 2024)

Startup Bootcamp Series, Ideation Workshops, Mentor-Mentee Matching Sessions, and Technical Skill Development Programs. Focus on building strong fundamentals and establishing clear business models.

Phase 2: Growth Acceleration (September - November 2024)

Pitch Perfect Sessions, Investor Connect Events, Market Validation Workshops, and Cross-Industry Collaboration Meetups. Emphasis on scaling strategies and market penetration techniques.

Phase 3: Innovation Showcase (December 2024 - February 2025)

Annual Innovation Summit, Demo Day Presentations, International Startup Exchange, and Technology Exhibition. Platform for showcasing breakthrough innovations and securing advanced funding rounds.

Phase 4: Global Expansion (March - May 2025)

International Market Entry Workshops, Global Partnership Conferences, Cross-Border Investment Forums, and Alumni Success Stories. Preparing startups for international markets and global scaling opportunities.
